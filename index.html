About Me ("Benny" Wayne Mattis)

Born and raised in Townsend, Massachusetts, I bring years of experience in customer service, disaster relief, food access, fundraising, volunteer recruitment, and analytic philosophy to my work as a designer, developer, and digital marketer. My interests lie primarily in the nonprofit sector and the video game industry: I aim to leverage automation, integration, and data-driven strategy to increase the impact of mission-driven nonprofits, and I aim to design and develop interactive stories which challenge players to see the world in a new light. My other pastimes include walking, weightlifting, meditating, and movie-watching.

I designed and coded this corner of cyberspace to function as a general introduction to myself and my work. I designed it from the ground up with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I created this "cyber-space-suit" image in Adobe Photoshop by combining an image of myself with a freely available astronaut image. The background image is another free resource that I altered in Photoshop for this website; feel free to observe it by clicking the "stargazing" button at the bottom-left of your screen. If you prefer reading dark text on a light background, you are welcome to click the "Light Mode" button at the bottom-right. Thank you for visiting; I hope you enjoy your stay!
