About Me

Welcome!

In my work as a web developer and IT consultant, I bring years of experience in the social sector to locate and implement solutions primarily for nonprofit clients. As an independent game developer and scholar of philosophy, I aspire to design and develop interactive games that provide a fun challenge while shedding some light on philosophical themes and questions.

This corner of cyberspace was created to function as an e-portfolio showcasing some examples of my work. I designed and developed its code with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Feel free to read some examples of my writing, web development coding examples, or games that I have developed by clicking the links in this site's header. Thanks for stopping by.

-Benny Mattis
