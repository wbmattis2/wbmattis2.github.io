About

Born and raised in Townsend, Massachusetts, I attended UMASS Amherst before traveling across the country for several years of full-time domestic volunteer service. The indispensability of technology for volunteer recruitment inspired me to pursue a career in web development and digital marketing. My other interests include philosophy (reading and writing), exercise (walking and weightlifting), movies, and game design.

I designed and coded this corner of cyberspace to showcase some of my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I also created the "cyber-space-suit" image in Adobe Photoshop by combining an image of myself with a freely available astronaut image. The background image is another free resource that I altered in Photoshop for this website. Don't forget to take a few moments to gaze into the dim green light of a virtual sky!
