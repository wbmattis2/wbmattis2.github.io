About

Born and raised in Townsend, Massachusetts, I went to UMASS Amherst before traveling across the country for several years of full-time domestic volunteer service. The indispensability of technology for volunteer recruitment inspired me to pursue a career in web design and development. My other interests include philosophy (reading and writing), exercise (walking and weightlifting), movies, and game design.

This corner of cyberspace was created primarily to serve as an ePortfolio of my work in web and software development. A few of my recent projects can be found under the "Projects" section. The "Resume" page outlines some previous roles I've filled over the years, and The "Coursework" page goes into more detail about some of the technical training I have undertaken.
