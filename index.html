About Me ("Benny" Wayne Mattis)

Born and raised in Townsend, Massachusetts, I bring years of experience in customer service, disaster relief, food access, fundraising, volunteer recruitment, and analytic philosophy to my work as a technical consultant and game designer. My interests lie primarily in nonprofits and small business: I aim to leverage automation, integration, and data-driven strategy to increase organizational impact, and I aim to design and develop interactive stories which challenge players to see the world in a new light. When I'm not working or learning new tools, some of my other pastimes include walking, weightlifting, meditating, and movie-watching.

This corner of cyberspace was created to function as a general introduction to myself and my work. I designed and developed its code from the ground up with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I created this "cyber-space-suit" image in Adobe Photoshop by combining an image of myself with a freely available astronaut image. The background image is another free resource that I altered in Photoshop and the Gnu Image Manipulation Program; feel free to observe it by clicking the "stargazing" button at the bottom-left of your screen, but watch out for space debris! Speaking of space debris, this site's sound effect is from Pixabay. Thanks for stopping by!
